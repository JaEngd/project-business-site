What is Chess Boxing?

The object of chess boxing is to either beat your opponent in alternate rounds of chess or boxing. Matches can be won from either discipline with the chess coming down to check mate or forfeit and the boxing in either a stoppage or points decision. Chess boxing began in Berlin in 2003 and has since spread around the world.
